My Work

New Business Initiative
2-sided marketplace for parking space

PARK is a platform (mobile application) that matches between supply and demand. The application will automatically match drivers with individually-owned parking spots in a safe, convenient, seamless way using a mobile application. PARK leverages the sharing economy to create a two-sided marketplace for parking (similar to Uber and Airbnb but for parking space). PARK’s core functionality is using complex algorithms to match drivers who are looking for parking with the best available spot that meets their criteria.

Product Strategy
Usecase development & GTM Strategy For Cortana Digital Assistant

Despite this precipitous level of growth that is projected to continue, skills and usage of digital assistants (DAs) on smart speakers, phones, and PCs are still developing. To illustrate, top task requests include playing music, getting the weather, getting the news, and checking the time. Microsoft has recognized that there is still a lot of room for a digital assistant with more advanced capabilities that can help save consumers time and boost their productivity by completing more complex tasks. In addition, no personal assistant has been able to truly master productivity in the workplace or cut across users' personal and professional lives. Therefore, the project was recommending how its artificial intelligence digital assistant team (Cortana) could bridge the gap for users between their work and home lives.
